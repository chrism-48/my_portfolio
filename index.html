About Me

I started out my career after graduating from Tri-County Technical College in an injection molding plant. At that plant I learned just about everything I could learn as pertains to injection molding. From there I stayed in that field from 1992 all the way through 2022. Now, I have enjoyed tinkering with computers over the years and I helped friends and family with their computer problems. I would take their laptops and whatever and try to fix them. I learned by screwing things up, but fixed a lot of problems. For the last few years, I have been working on programming. I learned the basics and started trying to build my own projects. I wished that computers would have been more prevalent when I was in school because I believe that is the route I would have taken. I did change careers in 2022 and got a computer job working in CAD / Engineering. I want to try to get involved in the IT industry doing what I love but I am not sure what path I want take. I really enjoy the design part of the web. Maybe a front-end developer job will be the route I take. Please go to my Projects page and visit my other links to see what I have been working on.
